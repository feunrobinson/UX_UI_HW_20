Stay 'n Play

Our mission was to create a brand-new mobile application that must solve a real need in users' everyday lives. We came up with our app Stay 'n Play, a virtual events app that allows users to create an all inclusive social experience that fosters connections with people.

During our user research, we discovered that our users are feeling isolated and are in need of meaningful connections due to the Pandemic. We believe that providing a virtual environment platform will be able create an all inclusive social experience for users to connect with family and friends.

User Research

Hypothesis

We think people are experiencing loneliness because virtual calls aren’t engaging enough.

Research Plan

Conduct an online survey, and 5 user interviews to gain insight on how people are navigating through the pandemic and how it has affected their ability to connect with people within and outside of their household. We also want to know what activities our users participate in and how that has changed.

Proto Persona

Interview Sample Questions


- Do you enjoy learning new things?
- What does a typical weekend look like since the pandemic?
- How do you feel about quarantining?
- What activities did you do before quarantine that you can’t do now?
- What does a typical weekend look like since quarantine?

Interview Insight


- Most users we interviewed preferred to connect through their phones
- All users have changed or cancelled holiday plans
- Most users are experiencing loneliness and disconnection from their communities
- Most users feel the pandemic will permanently change how we socialize moving forward
